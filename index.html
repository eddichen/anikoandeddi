travelling in budapest

Travelling to and from the airport

Travelling by taxi takes about 25mins to reach the city centre and from previous trips costs around £15 using Uber. If you're travelling with small children and require a child seat, there are a few companies which can arrange a car with a child seat if pre-booked in advance including Airport Transfers Budapest and Transfer Budapest Hungary

There are public transport options from the Airport into Budapest which involves getting the 200E bus from the airport to Kőbánya- Kispest Metro station and take the Metro from there into Budapest. The journey takes around 30mins. More information can be found on Budapest by Locals.

Public transport in Budapest

Getting around Budapest can be done on foot for most places, but Budapest has a good network of underground trains (Metro), trams and buses to get you around. The closest Metro station to Hotel Gellért is Szent Gellért tér on Metro line 4. There are tram and bus stops outside the hotel.

Tickets need to be validated by stamping or punching prior to the start of travel on metro lines M1, M2, M3 and M4, on other vehicles – on suburban trains within Budapest – immediately after boarding or departure. The validating machines located at the entrance of metro stations stamp the starting time of your trip onto your ticket.
